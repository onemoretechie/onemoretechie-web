## What's covered under Cloud Architecture

The design decisions that shape whether a system scales, survives, and stays operable — the Principal-level view of trade-offs.

### Core areas

- **System design** — walkthroughs of real scenarios (multi-tenant SaaS, event-driven backends, high-throughput APIs)
- **Multi-region** — active-active vs active-passive, replication trade-offs, DR playbooks
- **Event-driven patterns** — EventBridge, SNS/SQS fan-out, Kafka, event sourcing
- **Well-Architected reviews** — the six pillars, applied
- **Microservices vs monoliths** — when to pick each, and how to migrate between them
- **ADRs** — architecture decision record templates and real examples

### The frame

Architecture is a trade-off exercise. Any post that doesn't name what you're giving up hasn't earned its readership yet.
